# Business Insights

## Revenue Overview

- Total Revenue: 16.57 Million
- Total Orders: 98,665
- Total Customers: 95,419
- Total Sellers: 3,095

## Payment Analysis

- Credit Card is the dominant payment method.
- Credit Card generated 12.72 Million in revenue.
- Boleto is the second most used payment method.

## Product Analysis

Top Revenue Categories:

1. Health & Beauty
2. Watches & Gifts
3. Bed Bath Table
4. Sports & Leisure
5. Computers Accessories

Insight:

Health & Beauty is the highest revenue generating category.

## Geographic Analysis

Top Revenue States:

1. SP
2. RJ
3. MG

Insight:

The Southeast region dominates both revenue and customer volume.

## Customer Analysis

- Unique Customers: 95,419
- Average Orders Per Customer: 1.03

Order Distribution:

- 92,506 customers placed 1 order
- 2,673 customers placed 2 orders
- 192 customers placed 3 orders
- 29 customers placed 4 orders

Insight:

Approximately 97% of customers made only one purchase.

## Revenue Trend

- Revenue grew significantly during 2017.
- Revenue exceeded 1 Million per month during 2018.
- November 2017 showed a major spike.
- March 2018 was the highest revenue month.

Insight:

The marketplace experienced strong growth and scaling.
